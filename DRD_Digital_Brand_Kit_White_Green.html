01b — Logo Specifications

Clearspace

Always maintain a minimum clearspace of X around the mark — where X equals the height of the "D" in DRD. Never crowd the logo with other elements.

Minimum Size

Digital: 120px wide minimum for the full lockup. 32px for the mark-only icon. Print: 30mm wide minimum.

The Spiral Logic

The mark is built on Fibonacci rectangle proportions — each arc radius follows the sequence 1 · 1 · 2 · 3 · 5 · 8 · 13. The grid lines are the golden ratio construction guides.

Never Do This

Do not stretch, rotate, recolor outside the approved palette, add drop shadows, place on busy backgrounds, or use the wordmark without the mark.

Primary Pairing
Forest on White or Parchment for all body content. Grove for buttons, links, and the logo on light backgrounds.
Accent Usage
Leaf (#52A882) for hover states, highlights, stat numbers, and spiral detail lines. Never use as a full background.
Neutral Rule
All white is #FFFFFF. All off-white is Parchment. Body text is Forest. Grey (#6B7280) for secondary copy only.

05 — Brand Voice & Tone
Confident
DRD speaks with authority. Not arrogant — expert. We know what we're talking about and say it plainly.
"We don't guess. We diagnose, build, and deliver."
Warm
We're Mombasa-born. We talk to real business owners. No jargon walls. Approachable, honest, direct.
"Tell us where your business is. We'll show you what's possible."
Results-first
Every sentence earns its place. We lead with outcomes, back with proof. No fluff, no filler.
"3.1× qualified enquiries in 60 days. That's infrastructure."
Write Like This
✓  "Your website is costing you clients."
